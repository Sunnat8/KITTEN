Kitten

All kittens are born with blue eyes and will have their fully developed eye color at around 8 weeks old.

  • Scientific Name: Caracal
  • Average Length: 14 to 16 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 15.1 years
  • Habitat: Houses.

Cats were probably first kept because they ate mice, and this is still their main 'job' in farms throughout the world. Later they were kept because they are friendly and good companions. Their average lifespam is 15.1 years.
